Starting Aikido

Dublin Aikikai train 3 times a week and offer classes at different levels. We run regular beginners courses to teach the basic movements required to learn Aikido in a fun and safe manor. Please contact us for dates of the next course.

Classes are adapted to the level of the students attending, so we can accept new enrolements between courses. Please contact us for more information.

Classes take place in a friendly, open environment where beginners and more advanced students train together.

No special equipment is required - just loose, comfortable clothing such as tracksuit bottoms and a t-shirt. Normally once students commit to learning Aikido they purchase a keikogi. This is not compulsory but most students find them more comfortable. Standard jodo keikogi are available in all sizes in specialist martial arts shops and larger sports stores.
